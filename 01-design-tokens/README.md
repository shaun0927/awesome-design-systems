## Key Takeaways

- **Options first, decisions later**: Separate raw values (palette) from contextual choices (semantic tokens) to enable flexibility and reuse across themes
- **Naming is a system, not chaos**: Use structured 4-level naming (Namespace â†’ Object â†’ Base â†’ Modifier) and avoid ambiguous terms to ensure clarity across teams
- **Automation preserves intent**: Graph Engine and algorithmic approaches encode the "why" behind design decisions, making systems maintainable and scalable across platforms
- **Mathematical consistency beats pixel grids**: Proportional scales (modular scale ratios) provide perceptually balanced progressions that linear pixel increments cannot achieve
- **Future-proof your tokens**: Invest in W3C DTCG spec compliance and platform-agnostic formats (JSON) to ensure longevity and interoperability
